About this estimate

This calculator uses the Rockport Fitness Walking Test equation (Kline et al., 1987), which estimates VO₂max from a brisk one-mile walk on level ground. Walk as fast as you comfortably can, record your finishing time, and take your heart rate immediately upon finishing.

Like all submaximal field tests, it provides an estimate rather than a laboratory-measured value, and is most accurate for healthy adults walking at a genuine effort. Fitness categories shown are general references and vary by source.

This tool is for general information and is not a substitute for individualized medical advice. Talk with your physician before beginning or intensifying any exercise program.
